This collaboration blends Tata Tea's commitment to social awakening with the Plated Project's mission to combat hunger through art. Each exquisitely crafted tea-set showcases vivid illustrations that bring India's post-Independence history to life, celebrating iconic moments and events that have defined our nation's pride and prestige. Remarkably, 100% of the proceeds from each purchase will be donated to sponsor meals for underprivileged children, supporting their path to a brighter future.
